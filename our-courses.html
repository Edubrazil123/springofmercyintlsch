Our School

At Spring of Mercy International School, we believe that education goes beyond textbooks and classrooms; it is a journey of discovery, growth, and excellence. Our academic programme is designed to inspire curiosity, develop critical thinking, and build a strong moral and intellectual foundation in every child.

Our curriculum combines the best of Nigerian and international educational standards, ensuring that our students are well-prepared for both local and global challenges. With a team of qualified, passionate, and dedicated teachers, we create a learning environment that encourages creativity, discipline, and confidence.

Nursery Section

The Nursery Section provides a safe, joyful, and stimulating environment where children begin their educational adventure. Through play-based learning, songs, stories, and creative activities, we help children develop early literacy, numeracy, communication, and social skills. Our focus is to lay a strong foundation for a lifetime of learning while nurturing curiosity and imagination.

Primary Section

In the Primary School, we focus on academic excellence and character building. Our curriculum covers key subjects such as English Language, Mathematics, Basic Science, Social Studies, Civic Education, ICT, and Creative Arts. We emphasize interactive learning, problem-solving, and teamwork helping pupils build confidence and develop a love for learning. Teachers serve not only as instructors but also as mentors, guiding each child to achieve their full potential.

Secondary Section

The Secondary School continues the tradition of excellence by offering a broad and balanced curriculum that challenges students to think critically and act responsibly. Our academic programme prepares learners for national and international examinations while developing leadership, discipline, and integrity.

Subjects include Sciences, Arts, Commercial Studies, and Vocational Skills, supported by modern teaching methods, digital resources, and hands-on learning experiences. We also encourage participation in clubs, debates, science fairs, and community projects ensuring our students grow into well-rounded individuals equipped for higher education and global citizenship.

Our Commitment

At Spring of Mercy International School, we are committed to providing quality education that shapes the mind, builds the character, and inspires the future. Our goal is to raise learners who are not only academically sound but also morally upright, confident, and prepared to make a positive impact in the world.
