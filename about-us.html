About Us
  • Spring of Mercy International Schools is more than just a school; it is a family where students are empowered to dream, achieve, and make a difference.

Who we are

Welcome to Spring of Mercy International School, a leading centre of learning located in the heart of Obinze, Owerri, Imo State. We are committed to providing a world-class education that nurtures excellence, discipline, and character in every child.

At Spring of Mercy, we pride ourselves on creating a conducive learning
environment where students feel inspired, safe, and motivated to reach their
full potential. Our classrooms are well-structured, our facilities are modern,
and our atmosphere encourages creativity, innovation, and critical thinking.

Our team of highly qualified and dedicated teachers guides learners through a rich curriculum that balances academic achievement with moral values, personal growth, and global awareness. We believe in shaping well-rounded individuals who will thrive in an ever-changing world.

Why Choose Us

At Spring of Mercy International School, we go beyond academics to offer a holistic education that prepares students for life. Here’s why families trust us:

  • • Conducive Learning Environment: A safe, supportive, and inspiring atmosphere where every child feels valued and motivated to learn.
  • • Qualified and Passionate Teachers: Experienced educators dedicated to guiding, mentoring, and nurturing each learner.
  • • Balanced Curriculum: A rich blend of academics, moral values, creativity, and life skills designed to develop well-rounded individuals.
  • • Modern Facilities: Equipped classrooms, resource centres, and learning tools that make education engaging and effective.

Our Mission
