WHO WE ARE

Spring of Mercy International School stands proudly as one of the leading educational institutions in Owerri West, Imo State, known for its unwavering commitment to academic excellence, moral discipline, and holistic child development. At Spring of Mercy, we believe that every child is unique, gifted, and capable of achieving greatness when nurtured in a Conducive learning environment. Our school provides a blend of quality education, innovative teaching methods, and strong moral values, ensuring that our pupils are well-prepared to thrive in an ever-changing world.

OUR BUSS SERVICE
We

Have the Courage to have Convictions

At Spring of Mercy International School, we understand that safe
and reliable transportation plays a vital role in your child’s education. That’s why we provide a well-organized and efficient school bus service designed to make daily commuting convenient, comfortable, and secure for our students and parents alike. Our fleet of modern, well-maintained buses covers key routes across Owerri West and surrounding areas, ensuring that every child arrives at school and returns home safely and on time. Each bus is operated by a qualified driver and accompanied by a trained bus attendant who ensures the safety and proper conduct of pupils throughout the journey.
