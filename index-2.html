BUS SERVICE

At Spring of Mercy International School, we understand that safe and reliable transportation plays a vital role in your child’s education. That’s why we provide a well-organized and efficient school bus service designed to make daily commuting convenient, comfortable, and secure for our students and parents alike. Our fleet of modern, well-maintained buses covers key routes across Owerri West and surrounding areas, ensuring that every child arrives at school and returns home safely and on time. Each bus is operated by a qualified driver and accompanied by a trained bus attendant who ensures the safety and proper conduct of pupils throughout the journey.

SECTIONS IN SPRING OF MERCY INTL SCH'S.

Across all sections, Spring of Mercy International School remains committed to nurturing well-rounded learners academically sound, morally upright, and emotionally balanced. Each stage of learning is designed to help pupils grow in wisdom, confidence, and compassion.

Nursery Section

The Nursery Section lays the groundwork for academic and social success. Through creative play, storytelling, and interactive activities, we help children develop literacy, numeracy, and communication skills while fostering curiosity and confidence.

Focus: Early literacy, numeracy, social skills, creativity, and moral values.

Primary Section

Our Primary Section offers a balanced blend of academic excellence and moral training. With qualified teachers and a rich curriculum, pupils are guided to think critically, express themselves confidently, and develop a lifelong love for learning.

Focus: Core subjects (English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies), ICT, cultural studies, and character building.

Junior Secondary Section

The Junior Secondary Section prepares learners for higher education and leadership responsibilities. We focus on strong academic performance, discipline, and skill acquisition to help students excel in national and international examinations.
